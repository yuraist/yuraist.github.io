Release Notes

May 6, 2023

v1.1 – Discover Enhanced Experience

The latest update for the Nuta nutrition tracking iOS app focuses on enhancing user experience with a redesigned meal record creation and editing screen, improved main list, meal details, and statistics screens. Users can now add and edit custom meal types, export meal records in PDF, CSV, and plain text formats, and provide feedback easily through a new form to help improve the app.

April 6, 2023

v1.0 – Nutrition Journey Begins

Exciting news! Nuta is now available on the App Store! The app makes it easy to track your meals and reach your diet and weight goals. With meal record tracking, statistics, and reminders, you'll never forget to log your meals. Start your journey towards a healthier you - download Nuta today!
